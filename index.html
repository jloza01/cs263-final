Welcome to Data Transparency Network

In the modern digital age, the relationship between government entities and private tech companies has become increasingly intricate, particularly concerning the access to and handling of user data. The United States government possesses various mechanisms through which it can request data from these tech companies, a power often exercised for law enforcement, national security, or regulatory purposes. However, this authority is not without its controversies and legal debates, as it frequently implicates issues of privacy, civil liberties, and corporate transparency. Understanding the landscape of government data requests from private tech companies is crucial for comprehending the balance between security imperatives, individual rights, and corporate responsibilities in the digital age. This website aims to provide a central location to compare how the major tech companies are compling with these requests.
